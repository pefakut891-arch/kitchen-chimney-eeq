🌍 Why Air Flow Is Important

These days, Kitchen Chimneys are built close together. That means fresh air can’t always get in. If you don't get rid of cooking smells, the air inside can get bad over time. Breathing greasy air all the time can even make it hard to breathe well.

A good kitchen chimney fixes this by bringing in fresh air. It sucks up oil and smoke, keeps walls and furniture clean, and stops bad smells from spreading.

We want people to know how important this is—not just for feeling good, but for staying healthy and keeping your Kitchen Chimney in good shape.
